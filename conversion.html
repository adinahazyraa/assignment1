What is Conversion?

In general, "conversion" refers to the process of changing anything from one form, state, or unit into another. It is a wide concept that can be applied to a variety of sectors and situations.

Here are some of the examples :

Type of Conversion Description
Currency Conversion Converts one currency to another, such as RM to USD.
Measurement Conversion Converts one unit of measurement to another, such as inches to centimeters.
Temperature Conversion Converts one temperature scale to another, such as Celsius to Fahrenheit.
Character Conversion Converts characters from one encoding to another, such as ASCII to Unicode.
